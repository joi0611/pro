来源：2024 新疆真题
When we think of Xinjiang, sweet fruits, delicious foods and friendly people come to mind. Please follow me to something else about Xinjiang.

Muqam
Muqam is quite a special art form in China. During of years, Muqam used to be a good example of cultural exchange between the East the West. In order to it pass down, the government has started protection work. Even some universities have set up Muqam classes.

Xinjiang cotton
Xinjiang is home to cotton in China. It is for its long-staple cotton, many factories choose to buy Xinjiang cotton because its high quality. In the past, people mainly cotton by hand. Thanks the development of technology, more and more people use machines to do it now.

Qiemo County
Qiemo was one of the most important trade on the ancient Silk Road. Nowadays, Qiemo is still rich in culture and nature, like well-known jade, special folk customs and different natural wonders.

If you have a chance to visit Xinjiang, take it!
